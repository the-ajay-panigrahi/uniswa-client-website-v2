Kabuli Chickpeas

Kabuli Chickpeas (Dollar Grade) are large, round, cream-colored seeds. We grade them meticulously by diameter (mm count) for export to Europe, Middle East, and North America. Excellent cooking quality and high protein.

Quick Specs Summary
Origin: Madhya Pradesh / Maharashtra, India
Shelf Life: 24 Months
Packing: 25 kg / 50 kg PP Bags or Multi-layer Paper Bags
Storage: Cool, Dry Place
